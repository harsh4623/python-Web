ABOUT THE WEBSITE

E-commerce platforms have revolutionized the way we shop, and user reviews have become a cornerstone of the shopping experience. Our project, "ReviewSense: Decoding E-commerce Sentiments," is dedicated to unraveling the hidden insights within these reviews. By harnessing the power of natural language processing and data analytics, we delve deep into the sentiments expressed by shoppers on Flipkart.

Key features of our website

REVIEW PHONE DETAILS

Choose a specific smartphone model for analysis. Display some features of the mobile.

COMPARE TWO DIFFERENT PHONE

Select two different smartphone models for comparison.Conduct sentiment analysis on the reviews of both models.

FEATURE WISE MOBILE SELECTION

Extract features related to battery life,display quality and storage from the review text using relevant keywords.

SENTIMENT ANALYSIS

Analyze the distribution of sentiment labels (positive, negative, neutral) based on the review ratings.
